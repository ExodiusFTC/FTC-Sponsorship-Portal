# Matchmaker Agents

## Reviewer Agent
- **Role**: Vets the quality of the 'Custom Pitch' fields (custom pitch alignment, specific needs statement, local connection notes) before dispatching links to sponsors.
- **Workflow**: 
  1. Receives pending submissions.
  2. Analyzes the custom pitch fields for professionalism, relevance to the specific sponsor, and clarity.
  3. Approves or flags for coach edits.
